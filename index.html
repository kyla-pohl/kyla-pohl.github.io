Hi! I am a visiting assistant professor of mathematics at Colby College and an ABD PhD candidate at the University of Oregon. My advisor is Ben Young. I am an algebraic/enumerative combinatorialist studying Jack symmetric functions and hook length formulas.

Previously, I earned a Bachelor of Arts degree with a major in math and a concentration in Japan studies from St. Olaf College in Northfield, MN and a Masters degree in mathematics at the University of Oregon.
