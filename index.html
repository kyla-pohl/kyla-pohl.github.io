Hi! I am a PhD candidate in the mathematics department at the University of Oregon. My advisor is Ben Young. I am an algebraic/enumerative combinatorialist studying Jack symmetric functions and hook length formulas.

I expect to complete my degree in spring 2025. Previously, I earned a Bachelor of Arts degree with a major in math and a concentration in Japan studies from St. Olaf College in Northfield, MN and a Masters degree in mathematics at the University of Oregon.
